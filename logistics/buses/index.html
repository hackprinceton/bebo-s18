Buses

To help with transportation, we provide charter buses for hackers from Canada free of charge. Each bus holds about 50 people, and space is limited.

Eventbrite

In order to guarantee a spot, you will need to purchase a (fully-reimbursable) ticket through the relevant Eventbrite link below. The links will open at exactly 3 PM on March 7th, 2018. Spots are available on a first- come-first-serve basis. Please read the FAQ carefully. We will not reimburse any travel to the bus pickup location.
